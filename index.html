    About

    I'm 18 years old and from Sydney, Australia, I am currently studying Mechatronic Engineering and Computer Science at the University of New South Wales. I'm an undergraduate student who's passionate about what I do, looking for every opportunity I can to get experience!

    As a child I've always been interested in technology and computers, some of my earliest memories with a computer at 4 years old include Tux Paint and Full Tilt! Pinball. Since childhood I had an obsession with robotics and this carried with me throughout high school, especially when I was offered a coding elective, where I was able to build a robotic hand controlled by an arduino. In the year I was studying for my final high school exams I discovered a natural text to speech program that was trained using minimal data (15.ai). This piqued my interest in machine learning and algorithms, something I am still deeply fascinated by today.
